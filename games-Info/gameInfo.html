How to Play

Welcome to Blackjack Pro! Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over.

Rules:

  • Cards 2-10 are worth their face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10
  • Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever is more advantageous
  • Going over 21 results in a bust and loss of bet
